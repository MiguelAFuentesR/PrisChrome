Our Services

What We Offer

Digital Signature

By scanning your fingerprint, we provide a unique and secure way to digitally sign your artistic creations. This innovative approach ensures the authenticity and integrity of your artworks, providing peace of mind for artists and collectors alike

License

Manage your digital creations with ease using our Creative Commons licensing service. Customize licenses to specify usage terms for commercial or non-commercial purposes, with or without modifications. Simplify licensing so you can focus on creating and sharing content securely and legally

Blockchain

Safeguard your intellectual property, enhance transparency, and foster trust among users. Our service streamlines the process, enabling you to leverage the benefits of blockchain technology with ease

Noise

Enhance your data privacy with our noise injection service. By adding noise to digital files, we protect them from being used for artificial intelligence training purposes. Safeguard your sensitive information and maintain control over your digital assets

Recording in a database

Store your digital art securely with our database storage service. Safeguard your creative masterpieces by storing them in our reliable and secure database. Whether you're an artist, collector, or curator, our service ensures easy access to your digital artworks whenever you need them

Customizable Profile

Tailor your digital asset protection with our customizable profile service. Choose from a range of options including Creative Commons licensing, blockchain integration, noise injection, and digital fingerprint signing. Whether you're an artist, content creator, or business, our service empowers you to select the level of protection that best suits your needs
