About Us

PrismChrome

"Creative security, respect for artists: our solution combines digital innovation and copyright protection!"

We propose a solution that respects artists' copyright while promoting the creation of free content and securing digital content against plagiarism.

We are a group of students that in the face of the incessant increase of generative AI's that are trained with works of authors have decided to present a proposal to help artists to protect their works, we provide different innovative services with which the works of our clients will be a higher level of security.

Driven by the value of the artist we specialize in photogram work, however any artist will be warmly welcomed by our community and guided by the best specialists to protect their works.
